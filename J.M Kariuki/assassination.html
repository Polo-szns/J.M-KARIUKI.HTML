Mystery Around His Assassination

J.M. Kariuki was assassinated in 1975 under mysterious circumstances. His disappearance and subsequent murder sent shockwaves across Kenya.

His body was discovered in Ngong Forest, with evidence pointing to a politically motivated killing. Many believe that his outspoken criticism of corruption and injustice made him a target.

Despite multiple investigations, his assassination remains an unresolved case, fueling debates about political accountability in Kenya.
