Early Life & Education

J.M. Kariuki was born in 1929 in Nyeri, Kenya. Growing up during the colonial era, he experienced firsthand the injustices of British rule, which fueled his passion for equality and justice.

He attended King’s College Budo in Uganda, where he was exposed to progressive ideas and developed leadership skills. His education played a crucial role in shaping his political ideology and vision for Kenya’s future.
